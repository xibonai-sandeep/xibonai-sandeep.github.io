The XibonAI Story

From concept to launch in just 14 days, XibonAI is the result of three minds united by a singular vision: to revolutionize academic research with AI.

In a whirlwind two-week sprint, our team of three turned an ambitious idea into a powerful tool for researchers worldwide. We combined cutting-edge AI algorithms, robust backend architecture, and intuitive design to create a platform that simplifies complex research processes.

Our journey was fueled by late-night coding sessions, countless cups of coffee, and an unwavering belief in the power of technology to transform academic exploration.
