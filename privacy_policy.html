Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Xibon AI SuperBrain browser extension. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our extension. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the Xibon AI SuperBrain extension, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Website Content: We may capture, analyse, and store text, images, sounds, videos, or hyperlinks from the web pages you visit in order to provide the services offered by the extension, such as content summarisation, note-taking, and personalised learning assistance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the functionality of the Xibon AI SuperBrain extension.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personally Identifiable Information (PII) to outside parties. We may disclose your information in the following situations:

  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

5. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include internal reviews of our data collection, storage, and processing practices, as well as physical and technical security measures.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights concerning your data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@xibonai.com.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
